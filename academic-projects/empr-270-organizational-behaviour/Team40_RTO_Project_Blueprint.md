# Team 40 RTO Project Blueprint (EMPR 270)

## 1. Objective
Deliver a strong Team Project paper that analyzes one organization's RTO decision through:
- Organizational structure (Chapter 13)
- Organizational culture (Chapter 14)
- Change process implications (Chapter 15)
- Evidence-based recommendations linked to analysis

## 2. Deliverable Targets and Deadlines
- Team Charter: Friday, March 6, 10 PM
- Team Project Paper: Friday, April 3, 10 PM
- Team Member Contribution Evaluation: Friday, April 10, 10 PM

## 3. Candidate Organizations (Quick Screen)
Pick one with enough credible public information.

Option A: Amazon
- Pros: abundant sources, clear RTO policy signal, known culture artifacts and leadership principles.
- Risks: volume of info can cause scope creep.

Option B: Google/Alphabet
- Pros: clear RTO/hybrid policy conversation, strong culture tension angle.
- Risks: mixed business structure complexity.

Option C: Canadian backup (RBC, Shopify, etc.)
- Pros: local relevance.
- Risks: may have less transparent detail on structure/culture.

## 5. Paper Architecture (match assignment instructions)
1. Executive Summary (1 page)
2. Context (3-4 pages)
3. Analysis (4-5 pages)
4. Recommendations (~2 pages)

## 6. Quality Bar
- Every major claim has source support.
- Concepts are defined briefly and applied accurately.
- Recommendations are directly tied to analysis findings.
- Writing is integrated into one voice.
- APA references complete and consistent.

## 7. Team Workflow
- Weekly in-person meeting after Friday class.
- Mid-week async check-in by Tuesday night.
- Shared source tracker and outline maintained continuously.

## 8. Risk Controls
- Risk: weak evidence for structure/culture
  - Control: pre-screen org with at least 8-10 credible sources before final selection.
- Risk: fragmented writing voice
  - Control: one integration editor in final week.
- Risk: late bottlenecks
  - Control: internal deadline 48 hours before official deadline.
